FAQ

Frequently Asked

How do I start playing Wizards of Web3?

Simply sign up using your crypto wallet, customize your deck, and jump into your first card battle.

What are NFT cards and how do I get them?

NFT cards are unique collectible spells, creatures, and artifacts stored on the blockchain. Earn or trade them in-game or through marketplaces.

Can I earn crypto rewards by playing?

Yes! Compete in tournaments, win ranked battles, and complete quests to earn token rewards and rare NFTs.

Do I need a wallet to play?

Yes, a Web3 wallet like MetaMask is required to manage your NFT cards, game assets, and earnings.

Are all battles PvP?

No, we offer both PvP (player vs. player) and PvE (player vs. environment) modes to suit your style.

Can I trade or sell my cards?

Absolutely! All NFT cards can be traded or sold in our marketplace or any compatible NFT platform.

Is it free to play Wizards of Web3?

Yes! There’s a free-to-play version with starter cards. Premium content and NFTs can be unlocked through gameplay or purchase.
